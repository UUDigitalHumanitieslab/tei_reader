THE PROLOUG OF THE SAXT BUKE Pluto , thou patron of the deip Achiron , Fader of tormentis in thyne infernal see , Amyd the fludis , Stix and Flagitoin , Lethie , Cochite , the watyris of oblivie , With dolorus quhirling of furyus sustyris thre , Thyne now salbe my muse and drery sang : To follow Virgil in this dyrk poyse Convoy me , Sibil , that I ga nocht wrang .
